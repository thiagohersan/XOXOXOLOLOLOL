<h2>O Jogo</h2>
<p>“As our voluntary self-exposure through social-media and visible consumption becomes a kind of performance for the perpetual gaze of virtual others, we need to think about how this gaze inflicts how we find, measure and know one another, and also how it affects everything from our ability to access human warmth to our ability to access means of subsistence.”</p>
